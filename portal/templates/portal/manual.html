How to Use iConnect

Follow these simple steps to connect

1

Select a Plan

Choose from the available WiFi plans on the home page. Each plan shows the price and duration.

2

Insert Coins

Insert the correct amount of Philippine coins into the coin slot on the machine.

3

Get Connected

Once payment is detected, your device will automatically connect to the internet. Enjoy browsing!

4

Extend Your Time (Optional)

Insert more coins while your session is active. Enter the voucher code shown on the machine display to add more time.

FAQ

Why was I disconnected?

Your session may have expired. Check the timer on the session page. Insert more coins to continue.

The internet is slow. What should I do?

Try switching between 2.4GHz and 5GHz WiFi bands. If you're far from the router, 2.4GHz works better. If you're close, 5GHz is faster.

My coin was not detected. What do I do?

Make sure you're inserting a valid Philippine coin. Bent or damaged coins may not be accepted. Contact the admin for assistance.

Can I use GCash or digital payments?

Currently, iConnect only accepts Philippine coins (₱1, ₱5, ₱10, ₱20). Digital payments are not supported at this time.

WiFi Tip: If you're near the router, connect to the 5GHz network for faster speeds. If you're far away, use 2.4GHz for better range.
